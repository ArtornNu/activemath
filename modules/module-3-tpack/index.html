Planned outline

  • The three circles: Content Knowledge (CK), Pedagogical Knowledge (PK), and Technological Knowledge (TK) — and why their overlaps, not the circles themselves, are where good teaching lives.
  • Diagnosing a lesson: participants examine a mathematics activity and identify which knowledge domain it's weak in.
  • Case studies using this course's own toolkit: compare a Desmos activity, a Polypad activity, and a Graspable Math activity side-by-side and ask "what TPACK decision made this an appropriate — or inappropriate — tool choice?"
  • Bridge to Modules 4–5: TPACK becomes the lens for evaluating every tool used in the rest of the course.

This module benefits from being built after Module 4 is finished, since its strongest activity is likely to reuse the Desmos/Polypad/Graspable Math examples already on this site as the worked case studies.
